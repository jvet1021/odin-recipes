Grilled Chix

Description

Jonny's Famous Bolo, made with ground beef and mirepoix


Ingredients

  • 1# Chicken Breast
  • 1 btl marinade
  • salt, pepper, adobo, onion powder, garlic powder

Steps

  1. trim, clean chicken breasts
  2. marinate Chicken 1-5hrs
  3. preheat grill
  4. grill, turning every 4 minutes
  5. done when internal temp = 170F
